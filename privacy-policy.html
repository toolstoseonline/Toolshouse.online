We use collected information for the following purposes:

Our website displays advertisements from Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to:

Google may use data from your Google account and your activity across Google services to show you personalized ads. You can control ad personalization through:

  • Google Ads Settings: Visit myaccount.google.com/ads to manage personalized ads
  • Browser Privacy Settings: Restrict cookies and tracking
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) opt-out page

6.2 Disabling Personalized Ads

If you prefer not to see personalized ads, you can opt-out by:

  • Disabling cookies in your browser settings
  • Using privacy-focused browsers like Firefox or Brave
  • Installing browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger
