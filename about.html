Who We Are

Tools House Online was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: make essential file conversion and editing tools accessible to everyone, absolutely free, without any signup barriers.

We are a small team of developers and designers who believe that basic digital tools should not be locked behind paywalls or subscription fees. Everyone should be able to convert a PDF, resize an image, or trim a video without creating an account or paying monthly fees.
